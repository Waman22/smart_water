About Us

Our smart plant watering system is designed to make it easy for you to care for your plants, even if you have a busy schedule.
With its innovative technology, the system provides the perfect amount of water for your plants to thrive, without overwatering or underwatering them.
You can customize the settings to suit the specific needs of your plants, and receive alerts on your phone if anything needs your attention.
Our system also tracks environmental factors like temperature and humidity,and checks the soil moisture.
With our smart plant watering system, you can enjoy healthy, vibrant plants all year round, without any of the guesswork.
